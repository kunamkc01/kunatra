What Kunatra reflects back

Where you stand

A live net-worth and exposure snapshot — assets minus what's borrowed against them, with the returns you're actually earning (XIRR).

Signals, not advice

Plain-language flags when debt service, concentration, or leverage drift out of a healthy range. It points; you decide.

Household view

One picture for the family, with per-person net worth underneath. Give your spouse their own access, or manage a relative's on their behalf.

Asset operations

Rent, tenancy and true DSCR, work orders, vendors, inspections and a compliance calendar — the upkeep behind the numbers.

Acquire, don't data-enter

Add a property the way you got it — bought, inherited or gifted, in a year, at a price — and live in it or rent it. The math follows.

Built for India

Rupees, lakh and crore, TDS on rent and salary, EPF/PPF/NPS — the instruments and conventions you actually use.
